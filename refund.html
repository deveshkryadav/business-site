Refund & Cancellation Policy

At My Business, we strive to provide the best service possible. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a refund or cancellation under the following conditions:

  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of purchase.
  • Digital products once delivered are non-refundable unless defective.
  • Service cancellations must be requested at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days.

For any refund or cancellation requests, please contact us at support@mybusiness.com.
